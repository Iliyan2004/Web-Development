Art Studio (Auto CAD)

We have constructed a multipurpose building built for artists as a studio to make and showcase their work and for the public to witness work of art through open exhibition. A theater is provided for workshops, theatrical acts and seminars. For the convenience of the artists and public, a cafe and a parking area is provided outside and a designated prayer area is served by the first floor south balcony. We wanted to create a unique design inspired by several art museums and greenhouse buildings. Hence, a building of such architecture has no exact prior existing design or blueprints, showcasing the innovation an artist aspires to have.
